How to Access Microsoft Copilot

Microsoft Copilot is available for you to use today. Here's how you can get started:

Access Microsoft Copilot directly through your web browser by navigating to copilot.microsoft.com.

The first step is to open your preferred web browser. Once your web browser is open, type the following into the address bar: https://copilot.microsoft.com/ and then click 'Enter'. This will take you directly to the interface where you can begin your Copilot journey.

Different Ways to Access Copilot

  1. Through the Website: Visit https://copilot.microsoft.com/
  2. Through Bing: Access via https://www.bing.com/ and look for the Copilot icon near the search bar
  3. Through Microsoft Edge: Open Edge browser and look for the Copilot icon in the top right corner
  4. Mobile App: Download the Microsoft Copilot app on your phone

If you have a Copilot Pro subscription, access to Copilot extends beyond the basic features. With a subscription, you can use Copilot in select Microsoft 365 applications and get responses faster during peak times.

For businesses, Copilot is also integrated in platforms like Dynamic 365. Copilot can assist with things like data analysis, app development, and workflow automation.
