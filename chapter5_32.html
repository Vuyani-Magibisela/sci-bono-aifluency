Understanding GPTs and Plugins

GPTs and plugins are features in Microsoft Copilot that extend its capabilities and allow for more specialized assistance. While they serve different purposes, both enhance your experience with Copilot and enable it to better serve your needs.

Copilot GPTs are tailored versions for specific purposes, while plugins connect Copilot to external services.

GPTs vs. Plugins: What's the Difference?

Copilot GPTs are custom versions of Copilot tailored to a specific purpose. They're essentially specialized variants of the main AI model with particular skills or knowledge areas.

Plugins are applications that allow Copilot to connect with other services that specialize in specific tasks such as finding a flight or creating a song. They extend Copilot's capabilities by giving it access to external tools and data sources.

To start, let's take a closer look at what GPTs are and the different Copilot GPTs available to you.

What is a GPT?

Generative Pre-trained Transformers, or GPTs, are advanced AI models that have greatly changed how we understand and use AI. These large language models have been trained on vast amounts of text data, enabling them to understand and generate human-like text based on the prompts they receive.

The name breaks down into three key parts:

  • Generative: Emphasizes the model's ability to create original content
  • Pre-trained: Indicates the model has been trained on extensive datasets before being fine-tuned for specific applications
  • Transformer: Refers to the neural network architecture that enables these models to process and understand language

Exploring Copilot GPTs

Copilot is powered by a variant of the GPT model, and different versions are available. These versions, known as Copilot GPTs, are customized to assist you in specific tasks. Let's take a look at the Copilot GPTs available today.

Copilot offers specialized GPTs for different purposes like vacation planning, design, and cooking assistance.

Available Copilot GPTs

When you visit the Microsoft Copilot website and sign into your personal account, look at the top right of the screen. You'll see a list of all the GPTs available for you to use today:

  • Copilot: The general version that can assist with a wide range of topics
  • Designer: Helps with design-related tasks like creating images
  • Vacation Planner: Assists with planning holidays, providing destination ideas, and travel recommendations
  • Cooking Assistant: Helps with cooking-related tasks such as generating dinner ideas or finding recipes
  • Fitness Trainer: Assists with fitness-related tasks like generating personalized workout plans

To access any of these specialized GPTs, simply click on its name, and Copilot will change to that version. Each specialized GPT provides sample prompts relevant to its expertise to help you get started.

Using a Specialized GPT: Cooking Assistant Example

Let's use the Cooking Assistant as an example. After clicking on "Cooking Assistant," you'll see sample prompts for guidance. You can enter your own prompt like "Ingredients and instructions to make vegetarian bibimbap." The assistant will then provide a detailed list of ingredients and cooking steps.

For better readability, you can request the ingredients to be formatted into a table by asking "Put this information into a table format." Once the table is created, you have options to edit it in Excel, copy, or share it.

Enhancing Copilot with Plugins

Another feature available in Copilot are plugins. Copilot plugins are extensions that enhance the capabilities of Microsoft Copilot by connecting it to external services.

Plugins connect Copilot to external services like shopping, travel, music creation, and restaurant reservations.

These plugins allow Copilot to perform a wider range of actions and retrieve real-time information from third-party sources. They enhance Copilot's capabilities by connecting it to specific services that excel in particular domains.

Accessing and Using Plugins

To access the available plugins in Copilot:

  1. Click on the word "Plugins" located at the top right corner
  2. You'll find a list of available plugins that you can choose from
  3. You can select up to 3 plugins at one time
  4. If you want to change plugins after starting a conversation, simply select "New Topic" and choose different plugins

Some of the available plugins include:

  • Search: Enables Copilot to search the web for current information
  • Suno: Creates music and songs based on your descriptions
  • Instacart: Helps with shopping lists and grocery information
  • Kayak: Assists with travel planning and flight information
  • Klarna: Provides shopping assistance and product recommendations
  • OpenTable: Helps with restaurant reservations and recommendations

Practical Applications of GPTs and Plugins

Let's explore a practical example of using a plugin to enhance your Copilot experience. We'll use the Suno plugin, which allows you to create a song with lyrics right in Copilot.

The Suno plugin enables Copilot to create original songs complete with music and lyrics based on your prompts.

Creating Music with the Suno Plugin

To create a song using the Suno plugin:

  1. Turn on the Suno plugin from the plugins menu
  2. Type a prompt describing the song you want to create
  3. Being specific and sharing the musical genre will help Suno create the song you want
  4. For example: "Create a song about a tiny but mighty hero named Penny, a dog who guides her owner around the streets of Rome, enjoying the smell of trattorias and the relaxing sounds of fountains in melodic pop."
  5. Once submitted, lyrics will be generated and displayed, followed by the song itself
  6. You can then listen, download, and share the song

The combination of specialized GPTs and plugins makes Copilot highly versatile and adaptable to a wide range of needs. Whether you're planning a trip, creating a meal, generating a song, or designing an image, there's likely a GPT or plugin that can help you accomplish your goal more efficiently.

Choosing Between GPTs and Plugins

When deciding whether to use a specialized GPT or a plugin, consider the following:

  • Use a specialized GPT when you need conversational assistance focused on a particular domain, like cooking or vacation planning
  • Use plugins when you need to connect to external services or perform specialized tasks that require real-time data or specific tools
  • Combine both for complex tasks—for example, using the Vacation Planner GPT along with the Kayak plugin for a comprehensive travel planning experience

Copilot GPTs and plugins can make your experience with Copilot more personal. While GPTs provide conversational or image assistance, plugins add services tailored to your needs. Together, they create a customizable user experience that adapts to your specific requirements.
